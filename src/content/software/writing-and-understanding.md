title: "If I Can’t Write It, I Don’t Understand It"
desc: "Exploring how writing helps clarify thought and deepen understanding."
date: "2025-11-03"
slug: "writing-and-understanding"
---

Writing forces clarity.  
When you can’t explain an idea in words, it means you haven’t fully grasped it yet.

Use writing as a mirror — if your explanation is confusing, your understanding needs work.

> To understand deeply, write simply.
